How It Works

Deliberately simple

1

Tell us what you're making

A short message is enough. No pitch decks, no formal applications. Just describe what you're working on and why it excites you.

2

We decide quickly

No review boards. No six-week timelines. If the work is compelling, you'll hear back fast.

3

Money lands, you build

No strings attached. No equity. No reporting requirements. Use it however your project needs.

Ethos

Why this exists

Most funding is designed for people who already know how to get funded. The Mihai Fund is for everyone else — the person with a half-built prototype, the artist between projects, the maker who just needs a small push.

The fund grows with every donation. What starts as materials money, a domain name, or a train ticket to a workshop compounds into something bigger over time. Every contribution helps the next creator get started.
