Let the Matching Begin

We'll use the Gale-Shapley algorithm to find stable matches. Age gaps and attribute differences both affect compatibility.

How compatibility works:

For each pair, we compute max(0, 100 - 2 x |difference|) for each of the 6 attributes and average them. Then:

Age gap penalty: gaps over 5 years reduce compatibility by 1.5 per extra year (max -20).

Bonuses: Power couple (both ambitious) +4 | Emotional depth (both aware) +3 | Intellectual bond (both smart) +3 | Chemistry (both strong personality) +3 | Magnetic charm (high charisma) +3 | Classic attraction (beauty meets charisma) +2

The threshold determines the minimum compatibility for a match. Below it, people stay single.

The Simulation

Year by Year

Time passes. Relationships are tested. Some couples break up based on their breakup probability — a function of compatibility, age gap, emotional awareness, and relationship length. Singles re-enter the dating pool each year.

Breakup probability formula:

Base rate: 0.45 - (compatibility / 100) x 0.40
A 100% compatible couple has ~5% annual breakup risk. At 65% compatibility, it's ~19%.

Modifiers: Age gap > 10 years (+8%) | Low awareness in either (<35) (+6%) | Personality clash (diff > 40) (+5%) | Together 3+ years (-4%) | Together 5+ years (-8%)
