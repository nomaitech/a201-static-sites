What do we do

We build experiments at the edge of what feels real.

reaility is an experimental software lab. We build small, opinionated AI projects that probe the boundary between the real and the generated.

Every experiment we ship starts from the same place: what happens when AI gets good enough that you can't tell the difference? We build things that put that question in your hands and let you find out for yourself.

We work at four edges:

Perception

Games that test the limits of human ability to distinguish AI-generated output from the real thing.

Interaction

Experiments in how people behave when they're unsure whether they're engaging with a human or a machine.

Trust

Projects that examine what happens to trust, authenticity and evidence when AI can produce anything convincingly.

Live

Physical events that blend actors, special effects and AI to stage situations where the audience can't tell what's scripted and what's real.

We're a small team. We build what we think needs to exist, release it, and see what breaks.
