"The unexamined life
is not worth living." Socrates

Philosofriends began as a small gathering of curious people who wanted to think more rigorously about life's big questions — without the stuffiness of academia or the shallowness of casual conversation.

We meet once a month in London. Every event has a theme, a short reading to guide discussion, and plenty of time to let ideas breathe. No philosophy degree required. Just bring an open mind and a willingness to be wrong.

We are writers, lawyers, engineers, artists, students, and everything in between. What unites us is a belief that thinking carefully about life makes it richer — and that it's more fun to do together.
