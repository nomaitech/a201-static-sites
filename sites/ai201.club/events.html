How our events work

Free to attend

All AI201 events are free. We believe access to creative community shouldn't cost anything.

Bring something

You don't need to be an expert. A half-finished idea, a question, or just curiosity is enough.

All disciplines welcome

Artists, musicians, writers, coders. If you make things, there's a place for you here.

Community first

No sponsors, no sales pitches. Just makers talking to makers about the work.
