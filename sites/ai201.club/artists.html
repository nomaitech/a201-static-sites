// Visual chaos

Art made with
AI & attitude.

Generative art, AI-powered visuals, CSS sorcery, and creative coding experiments. We make pixels do things they weren't designed to do.

The ai201 art scene isn't about gallery walls or NFT floors. It's about making visual things that didn't exist before — using code, AI, and a healthy disrespect for conventional aesthetics. We generate, glitch, animate, and distort. We turn prompts into paintings and CSS into sculptures. If it looks weird and makes you feel something, it belongs here.

// What we make

Forms of creative chaos

All the ways our artists bend pixels to their will.

🌀

Generative Art

Code that creates art. Algorithms that produce unexpected beauty. Randomness as an aesthetic choice. Think Processing, p5.js, and shader magic.

🤖

AI-Generated Art

Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion — the new paintbrushes. Prompt engineering as an art form. The artist's role is curator, director, and chaos agent.

📺

Glitch Art

Intentional errors as beauty. Data bending, pixel sorting, compression artifacts elevated to fine art. The aesthetics of broken things.

🎨

CSS & Web Art

Pure CSS paintings. SVG animations. WebGL experiments. The browser as a canvas — no plugins, no frameworks, just the raw web platform pushed to its limits.
