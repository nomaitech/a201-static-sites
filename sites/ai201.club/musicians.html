// Sound experiments

Music made with
code & AI.

AI-generated beats, algorithmic compositions, sonic experiments, and sounds that shouldn't exist but absolutely do. The future of music is weird and we're here for it.

The ai201 music scene is where algorithms write melodies and prompts become productions. We're not replacing musicians — we're giving anyone the power to create sounds. Whether you're a seasoned producer using AI to push your boundaries or someone who's never touched a DAW but has a song in their head, this is your place. Describe it, generate it, ship it.

// The studio

Tools for AI musicians

Your digital studio. From AI generators to live coding environments — everything you need to make noise.

AI Gen
Suno
Generate full songs from text descriptions. Lyrics, melody, arrangement — all from a prompt. The vibecoder's jukebox.
AI Gen
Udio
Another AI music generator with remarkable quality. Specialises in realistic vocals and complex arrangements.
Live Code
Sonic Pi
Free live coding synth. Write Ruby code, hear music instantly. Used in schools and on stages worldwide. Beginner-friendly.
Live Code
TidalCycles
Pattern-based live coding for music. Create complex rhythms and textures with minimal code. The algorave weapon of choice.
DAW
Ableton Live + AI
Industry-standard DAW with growing AI plugin ecosystem. Max for Live patches, AI-powered effects, and generative MIDI tools.
Free
Tone.js
Web Audio framework for creating interactive music in the browser. Build synths, sequencers, and audio experiments with JavaScript.
AI Gen
AIVA
AI composer for film scores, game music, and emotional soundtracks. Trained on classical compositions. Surprisingly moving.
Live
SuperCollider
The OG audio programming platform. Powerful, complex, and beloved by sound artists and academic composers worldwide.
// Getting started

Make your first track

Zero musical knowledge required. Seriously.

01

Pick your vibe

What mood are you in? Chill lo-fi beats? Aggressive industrial? Whimsical 8-bit? Your vibe is your genre. Don't overthink it.

02

Describe it to an AI

Open Suno, Udio, or any AI music tool. Describe what you want: "dreamy synthwave with a melancholic piano melody and lo-fi drums." Hit generate. Be amazed or horrified — both are valid outcomes.

03

Iterate and layer

Like what you hear? Great. Hate it? Generate again with different words. Want to go deeper? Import the stems into a DAW or layer multiple generations. The AI is your session musician.

04

Share the sound

Post it in the ai201 community. Add it to a vibecoded website. Score your weird web experiment. The internet needs more original music and you just made some.
