What we are

Humans first.
AI as the amplifier.

We are a collective of curious people — artists, musicians, writers, coders, and makers — who gather in London to push creative work further using AI as a companion, not a replacement.

We believe the best things are still made by people with something to say. AI doesn't write the song, paint the picture or tell the story. It's the instrument. We play it.

Every month we meet, build, share and critique. We run workshops, host events, and ship things. The results live here.
