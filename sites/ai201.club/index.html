// What is this

Not your average
coding meetup.

ai201.club is a community of builders, artists, and general internet weirdos based in London. We don't build startups. We don't optimise funnels. We make websites that make people laugh, think, or just go "...what?"

Every day, someone posts a creative challenge. You build something. You ship it. The internet gets a little weirder. That's the whole deal.

⚡

Daily Creative Challenges

A new prompt drops every morning. Build a website in a day. No rules, no judges, just vibes.

🧪

Weird Web Experiments

Push CSS to its limits. Abuse every API. Make the browser do things it was never meant to do.

👾

Community of Builders

201+ creative coders who hype each other up, share tricks, and collectively confuse the internet.

🍺

IRL Meetups in London

Monthly sessions at pubs, studios, and co-working spaces across London. Laptops and pints welcome.
