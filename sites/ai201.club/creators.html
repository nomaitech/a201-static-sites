People

The humans behind
the work.

64 members across six disciplines. Artists, musicians, coders, writers, designers and makers — united by curiosity about what AI makes possible.

We don't have membership tiers or application forms. You show up, you make something, you belong. AI201 is whoever turns up — which has so far included a classically-trained composer who makes AI-driven drum machines, a novelist who uses language models as a first-draft partner, and a data scientist who makes generative botanical illustrations on weekends.
