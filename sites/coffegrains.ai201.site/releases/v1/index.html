London Brewing Rituals

06:30 - Borough Market

Use 18g grains to 300g water. Pour in concentric circles. Let the first bloom breathe for exactly 35 seconds.

13:10 - Clerkenwell Desk Brew

Grind one click finer than usual, lower water temp to 91 C, and finish with a quick swirl to sharpen acidity.

21:45 - Thames Night Cup

Cold-steep coarse grains for 14 hours, add tonic and orange zest for a sparkling night profile.
